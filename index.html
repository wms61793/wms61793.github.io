Call of Duty League

The Call of Duty League is a professional Esports league with a prize pool of $5,000,000. The league consists of 5 major events, with each team competing in 5 matches every week to determine seeding for the major tournament at the end of every month. The league houses 12 teams from 4 different countries, each competing for a chance at the $500,000 grand prize up for grabs at each of the 5 majors, and $1,200,000 to the winner of the big tournament at the end of the season.
